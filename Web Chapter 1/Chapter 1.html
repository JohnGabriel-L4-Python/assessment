Chicken Adobo





  •     Hailed as the Philippines' national dish, Chicken Adobo captivates with its harmonious blend of sweet, savory, and tangy flavors. Chicken adobo is a beloved Filipino dish that boasts a rich and savory flavor profile, making it a favorite among both locals and international food enthusiasts.This versatile dish can be enjoyed as a hearty main course with steamed rice or as a flavorful addition to various dishes, showcasing its universal appeal. This short, simple, and easy guide will let you crave this dish for ages!



Chicken Adobo Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs - cannot substitute with breast, need the fat to transform sauce into a glaze;
  • Neutral oils - vegetable oil or canola oil
  • Soy sauce – all purpose or light soy sauce. NOT dark soy sauce.
  • White vinegar –
  • Onion and garlic
  • Black whole peppercorns
  • Sugar – brown best
  • Bay leaves – fresh or dried, (recommended)
  • Green onion – optional garnish

Simple and easy steps to cook chicken adobo!

  1. Cook Onions - On your stove top bring a large skillet to medium heat, add oil. Followed by sliced onions and cook until translucent.
  2. Brown the Chicken - Move the onions aside and place chicken thighs skin side down. Allow the skin to sear until golden brown, about 3-5 minutes. The best way to brown chicken is to not touch it after you place it in the pan. If you constantly move the chicken around, it won't brown well.
  3. Add Seasoning - Pour in soy sauce, garlic, black peppercorns, bay leaves and lastly white vinegar. For best results, do not stir the sauce as this prevents the acid from fully evaporating. This is a tip that I learned.
  4. Simmer the Chicken - Bring to a boil on medium-high heat. Then cover and reduce to a rolling simmer or low-medium heat for 10 minutes.
  5. Flip Chicken Over - Uncover the lid and flip the chicken over so the skin is facing up. Cover and simmer for another 10 minutes
  6. Simmer Sauce - Uncover the lid and continue to simmer for 3-5 minutes so the sauce can thicken a little bit.
  7. Garnish with green onions, emove off heat to serve & enjoy!
