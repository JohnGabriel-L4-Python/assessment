Breaking Bad

"Breaking Bad" follows the transformation of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer, into a ruthless drug lord. After being diagnosed with terminal lung cancer, Walter partners with former student and small-time methamphetamine manufacturer Jesse Pinkman to secure his family's financial future. As they enter the drug trade, Walter's descent into the criminal underworld leads to increasingly dangerous situations and moral compromises.
